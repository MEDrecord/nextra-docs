# Account and Settings

Manage your account, preferences, and security settings.

## Profile Settings

### Update your profile information

1. Click your avatar in the top right corner
2. Select **Profile Settings**
3. Update your name, email, or phone number
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Change your profile photo

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click on your current avatar
3. Upload a new image (JPG, PNG, max 2MB)
4. Adjust the crop area if needed
5. Click **Save**

## Security Settings

### Change your password

1. Go to **Settings** then **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Enable two-factor authentication

1. Go to **Settings** then **Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes securely

### View login history

1. Go to **Settings** then **Security**
2. Scroll to **Login History**
3. Review recent login attempts
4. Report any suspicious activity

## Notification Preferences

### Configure email notifications

1. Go to **Settings** then **Notifications**
2. Toggle email notifications on/off for each category
3. Set digest frequency (immediate, daily, weekly)
4. Click **Save Preferences**

### Configure in-app notifications

1. Go to **Settings** then **Notifications**
2. Choose which events trigger in-app alerts
3. Set notification sound preferences
4. Click **Save Preferences**

### Set quiet hours

1. Go to **Settings** then **Notifications**
2. Enable **Quiet Hours**
3. Set start and end times
4. Choose which notification types to suppress
5. Click **Save**

## Data and Privacy

### Export your data

1. Go to **Settings** then **Privacy**
2. Click **Export My Data**
3. Select the data categories to include
4. Click **Generate Export**
5. Download the ZIP file when ready

### Delete your account

1. Go to **Settings** then **Privacy**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Read the warning about data deletion
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Click **Permanently Delete**

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed.

## Organization Settings

*These settings are only available to administrators.*

### Manage team members

1. Go to **Organization** then **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Assign a role
5. Click **Send Invitation**

### Configure roles and permissions

1. Go to **Organization** then **Roles**
2. Select a role to edit or create a new one
3. Configure permissions for each feature area
4. Save changes
