# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about our products and services.

## General

### What is HealthTalk?

HealthTalk is a secure healthcare communication platform that enables healthcare providers to communicate with patients through multiple channels while maintaining full compliance with healthcare regulations.

### How do I get started?

Visit the [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/introduction) guide for step-by-step instructions on setting up your account and configuring your first communication workflows.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We implement industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, compliance with ISO 13485, NEN 7510, and GDPR. See our [Security](/developer/architecture/security) documentation for details.

## Account

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### How do I update my profile?

Navigate to Settings in your account menu. From there you can update your name, email, notification preferences, and other profile information.

### Can I have multiple users on one account?

Yes, our platform supports multi-user accounts with role-based access control. Contact your administrator to add new users or adjust permissions.

## Messaging

### What channels are supported?

We support SMS, email, in-app notifications, and patient portal messaging. See [Patient Communication](/docs/user-guide/patient-communication) for details.

### Are messages encrypted?

Yes, all messages are encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

### How do I create a message template?

Visit [Message Templates](/docs/user-guide/message-templates) for instructions on creating and managing reusable message templates.

## Billing

### How do I view my invoices?

Invoices are available in your account settings under the Billing section. You can download PDF copies of all historical invoices.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and invoicing for enterprise accounts.
