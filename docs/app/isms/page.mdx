## Scope

The ISMS applies to:

- **MedSafe PGO** - Personal health environment
- **HealthTalk** - Healthcare communication platform
- **ClubDiabetes** - Patient community
- **Supporting infrastructure** - Servers, networks, cloud services
- **Personnel** - All employees and contractors
