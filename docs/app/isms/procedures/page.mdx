## How Procedures Work

### 1. Policy to Procedure Mapping

Each procedure implements one or more policies:

```
Policy (WHAT) → Procedure (HOW) → Work Instruction (STEP-BY-STEP)
```

### 2. Document Control

All procedures include:
- **Version number** - Tracks document revisions
- **Approval status** - Draft, Under Review, or Approved
- **Approver** - Person responsible for approval
- **Review date** - When the procedure was last reviewed

### 3. Related Resources

Each procedure links to:
- Related policies (in Helpdesk)
- Related Annex A controls
- Templates and forms (in SharePoint)
- Training materials (in MS Teams)
