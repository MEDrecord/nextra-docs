## Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for retaining and disposing of MEDrecord information to ensure compliance with legal requirements while minimizing data storage risks.

## Scope

This policy applies to all MEDrecord information regardless of format:
- Electronic records
- Paper documents
- Emails and communications
- Database records
- Backup media
- Application logs

## Policy Statement

### Retention Principles

1. **Legal Compliance** - Retain data as required by law
2. **Business Need** - Keep data needed for operations
3. **Data Minimization** - Delete data when no longer needed
4. **Secure Disposal** - Destroy data securely when retention ends

### Retention Schedule

#### Healthcare Data

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Patient records (adults) | 20 years after last contact | WGBO |
| Patient records (minors) | Until age 35, minimum 20 years | WGBO |
| Medical device records | 15 years | MDR |
| Clinical trial data | 25 years | GCP |

#### Financial Records

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Financial statements | 7 years | Fiscal law |
| Invoices | 7 years | VAT regulations |
| Tax records | 7 years | Tax law |
| Contracts | 7 years after expiry | Civil code |

#### HR Records

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Employment contracts | 7 years after end | Labor law |
| Payroll records | 7 years | Tax law |
| Application records (hired) | Duration of employment + 2 years | GDPR |
| Application records (not hired) | 4 weeks (or with consent 1 year) | GDPR |
| Training records | 5 years | NEN 7510 |

#### IT and Security

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Security logs | 2 years | ISO 27001 |
| Access logs | 2 years | NEN 7510 |
| Audit trails | 7 years | Compliance |
| Incident records | 5 years | ISO 27001 |
| Backup data | Per backup policy | Business need |

#### Business Records

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Contracts | 7 years after expiry | Civil code |
| Meeting minutes | 10 years | Corporate governance |
| Policies and procedures | Active + 5 years | Compliance |
| Email (business critical) | 7 years | Legal hold |
| Email (general) | 3 years | Business need |

### Legal Hold

When litigation or investigation is anticipated:
- Normal deletion schedules are suspended
- Affected data must be preserved
- Legal department issues hold notices
- Hold remains until released by Legal

### Data Disposal

#### Secure Deletion Methods

| Media Type | Disposal Method |
|------------|-----------------|
| Paper | Cross-cut shredding (DIN 66399 Level P-4) |
| Hard drives | Degaussing + physical destruction |
| SSDs | Cryptographic erasure + destruction |
| Cloud storage | Verified deletion + confirmation |
| Optical media | Physical destruction |
| Backup tapes | Degaussing + destruction |

#### Disposal Documentation

Document all disposal activities:
- Date of disposal
- Description of records
- Disposal method
- Personnel involved
- Verification of completion

#### Third-Party Disposal

When using disposal vendors:
- Use certified vendors only
- Require certificates of destruction
- Include in supplier contracts
- Audit vendor compliance

### Personal Data (GDPR)

Additional requirements for personal data:

- **Purpose limitation** - Delete when purpose fulfilled
- **Data subject requests** - Respond to deletion requests within 30 days
- **Retention justification** - Document why data is kept
- **Regular review** - Assess retention needs annually

#### Deletion vs. Anonymization

When possible, anonymize data instead of deletion to:
- Preserve statistical value
- Enable historical analysis
- Reduce deletion complexity

Anonymization must be irreversible.

### Application Data Management

#### Database Records

- Implement automated retention in applications
- Archive old data before deletion
- Maintain audit trails of deletions
- Document retention logic in applications

#### File Storage

- Use folder structures aligned with retention
- Implement automated cleanup where possible
- Regular reviews of shared drives
- Clear ownership and classification

### Exceptions

Exceptions to retention schedules require:
- Written business justification
- Legal review
- Security Officer approval
- Documented approval
- Regular review of exception
