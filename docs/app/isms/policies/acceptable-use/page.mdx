## Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of MEDrecord information technology resources to protect employees, partners, and the company from harm resulting from misuse.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who use MEDrecord IT resources, including:
- Computers and laptops
- Mobile devices
- Network resources
- Email and messaging systems
- Internet access
- Software and applications
- Data and information

## Policy Statement

### General Use

MEDrecord IT resources are provided primarily for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted provided it:
- Does not interfere with work duties
- Does not violate any policies
- Does not consume excessive resources
- Does not expose the company to risk

### Acceptable Activities

**Permitted:**
- Business communications via approved channels
- Research related to job responsibilities
- Professional development and training
- Limited personal use during breaks
- Use of approved software and services

### Prohibited Activities

**The following are strictly prohibited:**

**Security Violations:**
- Attempting to bypass security controls
- Sharing login credentials
- Installing unauthorized software
- Connecting unauthorized devices
- Accessing systems without authorization

**Illegal Activities:**
- Downloading pirated software or media
- Distributing malware or hacking tools
- Engaging in fraud or theft
- Violating copyright or intellectual property
- Any activity that violates Dutch or EU law

**Inappropriate Content:**
- Accessing, storing, or distributing pornography
- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination
- Offensive, racist, or hateful content
- Gambling websites

**Data Misuse:**
- Unauthorized access to patient/healthcare data
- Exfiltrating company or customer data
- Sharing confidential information externally
- Using data for personal gain

**Resource Abuse:**
- Cryptocurrency mining
- Running personal businesses
- Excessive bandwidth consumption
- Storing large personal files

### Email and Communication

**Do:**
- Use professional language
- Protect sensitive information
- Verify recipients before sending
- Use encryption for confidential data
- Report suspicious emails (phishing)

**Do Not:**
- Send confidential data to personal email
- Open attachments from unknown sources
- Click links in suspicious emails
- Use company email for personal matters
- Auto-forward company email externally

### Internet Use

**Permitted:**
- Work-related research
- Professional networking (LinkedIn)
- News and reference sites
- Approved cloud services

**Monitored/Restricted:**
- Social media (limited personal use)
- Streaming services
- Personal shopping
- File sharing sites

**Blocked:**
- Known malware sites
- Adult content
- Gambling
- Anonymizers/proxies

### Software and Applications

- Only use software approved by IT
- Do not install browser extensions without approval
- Keep all software updated
- Report licensing concerns to IT
- Do not use cracked or pirated software

### Social Media

When using social media:
- Do not share confidential company information
- Do not speak on behalf of MEDrecord without authorization
- Be respectful and professional
- Clearly state personal opinions are your own
- Report any MEDrecord mentions that concern you

### Monitoring

MEDrecord reserves the right to monitor:
- Network traffic
- Email communications
- Internet usage
- System access logs
- Device activity

This monitoring is conducted to:
- Ensure policy compliance
- Protect company assets
- Investigate security incidents
- Meet legal and regulatory requirements

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Employees | Follow policy, report violations |
| Managers | Ensure team awareness, address violations |
| IT Team | Implement controls, monitor compliance |
| Security Officer | Define policy, investigate incidents |
| HR | Address disciplinary matters |

## Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Temporary suspension of IT access
- Disciplinary action up to termination
- Legal action if laws are violated
- Civil liability for damages caused

## Reporting

Report policy violations or concerns to:
- Your direct manager
- IT Support
- Security Officer
- HR (for sensitive matters)
- Anonymous reporting via compliance hotline
