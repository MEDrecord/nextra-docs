## Purpose

This overarching Information Security Policy establishes MEDrecord's commitment to protecting information assets and defines the framework for our Information Security Management System (ISMS).

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All MEDrecord employees, contractors, and third parties
- All information assets regardless of form or format
- All information systems and processing facilities
- All locations where MEDrecord information is handled

## Management Commitment

MEDrecord's management is committed to:
- Protecting information assets from all threats
- Maintaining confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information
- Complying with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements
- Continuously improving the ISMS
- Providing resources for information security initiatives
- Promoting security awareness throughout the organization

## Information Security Objectives

| Objective | Target | Measure |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Maintain ISO 27001 certification | Annual certification | Certification status |
| Maintain NEN 7510 compliance | Continuous | Audit results |
| Security incident reduction | <5 high/critical per year | Incident count |
| Security awareness | 100% staff trained | Training completion |
| Vulnerability remediation | Critical <24h, High <7d | Remediation time |
| Availability | 99.9% uptime | Service availability |

## Security Principles

### Confidentiality
Information is accessible only to those authorized to access it.

### Integrity
Safeguarding the accuracy and completeness of information and processing methods.

### Availability
Ensuring authorized users have access to information when required.

## Risk Management

MEDrecord implements a risk-based approach to information security:

1. **Identify** assets and threats
2. **Assess** likelihood and impact
3. **Treat** risks (mitigate, accept, transfer, avoid)
4. **Monitor** and review risks continuously

See [Risk Register](/isms/risks) for identified risks and treatments.

## Security Awareness

All personnel must:
- Complete security awareness training upon joining
- Complete annual refresher training
- Report security concerns and incidents
- Stay informed of policy updates

## Incident Management

Security incidents must be:
- Reported immediately via [Report Incident](/isms/report-incident)
- Assessed and prioritized
- Contained and resolved
- Analyzed for lessons learned

See [Incident Management Policy](/isms/policies/incident-management).
