## Risk Assessment Methodology

### Likelihood Levels

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| L (Low) | Unlikely to occur |
| M (Medium) | May occur occasionally |
| H (High) | Likely to occur |

### Impact Levels

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| L (Low) | Minor impact |
| M (Medium) | Moderate impact |
| H (High) | Severe impact |

### Exposure Matrix

| Likelihood / Impact | L | M | H |
|---------------------|---|---|---|
| H | M | H | H |
| M | L | M | H |
| L | L | L | M |
