## Risk Categories

### Social Engineering and Human Factors

Risks related to human behavior and social manipulation.

- [2019-04 Social engineering](/isms/risks/2019-04-social-engineering) - Employees give access to unauthorized people
- [2019-06 Account sharing](/isms/risks/2019-06-account-sharing) - Ex-employee damages or leaks information
- [2019-29 Usage of weak passwords](/isms/risks/2019-29-weak-passwords) - Password hacked or guessed

### Technical and Infrastructure

Risks related to systems, software, and technical vulnerabilities.

- [2019-05 Security vulnerability exploited](/isms/risks/2019-05-security-vulnerability) - Vulnerability in underlying technology
- [2019-11 Loss of cryptographic keys](/isms/risks/2019-11-loss-cryptographic-keys) - Data inaccessible due to key loss
- [2019-14 E-mail intercepted](/isms/risks/2019-14-email-intercepted) - Insecure SMTP communication
- [2019-28 Improper configuration](/isms/risks/2019-28-improper-configuration) - Unnecessary services or ports open

### Third Party and Supply Chain

Risks related to suppliers and external dependencies.

- [2019-08 Insufficient supplier control](/isms/risks/2019-08-insufficient-supplier-control) - Supplier does not meet requirements

## Risk Assessment Methodology

### Likelihood Levels

| Level | Code | Description | Frequency |
|-------|------|-------------|-----------|
| Low | L | Unlikely to occur | Less than once per year |
| Medium | M | May occur occasionally | 1-4 times per year |
| High | H | Likely to occur | More than 4 times per year |

### Impact Levels

| Level | Code | Description | Business Impact |
|-------|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Low | L | Minor impact | Minimal disruption, no data loss |
| Medium | M | Moderate impact | Some disruption, limited data exposure |
| High | H | Severe impact | Major disruption, significant data breach |

### Exposure Matrix

The exposure (residual risk) is calculated based on likelihood and impact:

|  | **Impact: Low** | **Impact: Medium** | **Impact: High** |
|--|-----------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| **Likelihood: High** | Medium | High | High |
| **Likelihood: Medium** | Low | Medium | High |
| **Likelihood: Low** | Low | Low | Medium |

### Treatment Options

| Treatment | Description | When to Use |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Accept** | Accept the risk without additional measures | Low exposure, cost of treatment exceeds benefit |
| **Mitigate** | Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact | Medium/high exposure, feasible controls available |
| **Transfer** | Transfer risk to third party (insurance, outsourcing) | High impact, cannot be mitigated internally |
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk by removing the activity | Very high exposure, no feasible mitigation |

## Review Schedule

- **Quarterly**: Review high and medium exposure risks
- **Annually**: Full risk assessment review
- **Ad-hoc**: After security incidents or significant changes
