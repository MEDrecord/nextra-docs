# A.6.1.2 Segregation of duties

## Control Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Requirement** | Conflicting duties and areas of responsibility shall be segregated to reduce opportunities for unauthorized or unintentional modification or misuse of the organization's assets. |
| **Applicable** | Yes |
| **Status** | Implemented |

## Implementation

Duties are segregated to ensure that:

- No single individual has complete control over critical processes
- Changes require multiple approvals
- Access rights are based on job function
- Developers cannot deploy to production without review

## Check Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Owner** | Information Security Officer |
| **Frequency** | Quarterly |
| **Instruction** | Review access rights and approval workflows to ensure proper segregation |
