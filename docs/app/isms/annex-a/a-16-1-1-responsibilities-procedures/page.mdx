# A.16.1.1 Responsibilities and procedures

## Control Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Requirement** | Management responsibilities and procedures shall be established to ensure a quick, effective and orderly response to information security incidents. |
| **Applicable** | Yes |
| **Status** | Implemented |

## Implementation

- Incident response procedure documented and approved
- Roles and responsibilities clearly defined
- Escalation paths established
- Regular incident response drills conducted

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Information Security Officer | Overall incident coordination |
| Lead Developer | Technical investigation and remediation |
| Management | Communication and business decisions |

## Check Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Owner** | Information Security Officer |
| **Frequency** | Quarterly |
| **Instruction** | Review incident response procedures and conduct tabletop exercises |
