## General Information

### Medical Device Regulation (MDR)

We do not generate, interpret, process and mutate medical data. We simply display it. This means we are not a risk class two medical device and we do not have to comply with the MDR.

However, we plan to be ISO 13485 certified and future software might become a risk class two medical device. In that case, we will make sure to comply with the MDR.

## Generic Legal Requirements

| Requirement | Reference | System/Process | Compliant | To Do |
|-------------|-----------|----------------|-----------|-------|
| Asking permission before placing cookies | [GDPR/ePD](https://gdpr.eu/cookies/) + [UAVG](https://wetten.overheid.nl/BWBR0040940/2018-05-25) | Web sites | Yes | - |
| Rights of data subjects | [GDPR articles 15-23](https://gdpr-info.eu/chapter-3/) + UAVG | MedSafe PGO, ClubDiabetes, HealthTalk | Yes | - |
| Privacy policy | [GDPR articles 12, 13 and 14](https://gdpr-info.eu/chapter-3/) + UAVG | Public websites | Yes | - |
| Maintaining records of processing activities | [GDPR article 30](https://gdpr-info.eu/art-30-gdpr/) + UAVG | ISMS | Yes | - |
| Maintaining data processing agreements | GDPR | Supplier contracts | Yes | - |
| Data Protection Impact Assessment | GDPR | High-risk processing | Yes | - |
| Appointing a Data Protection Officer | GDPR | Organization | Yes | - |
