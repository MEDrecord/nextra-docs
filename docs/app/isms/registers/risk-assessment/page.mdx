This register contains all identified risks and their treatment status.

## Risk Categories

- **Confidentiality** - Risks to data confidentiality
- **Integrity** - Risks to data integrity
- **Availability** - Risks to system availability

## Treatment Options

| Treatment | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **ACCEPT** | Risk is accepted within tolerance |
| **MITIGATE** | Risk is reduced through controls |
| **TRANSFER** | Risk is transferred (e.g., insurance) |
| **AVOID** | Risk is avoided by eliminating the activity |
