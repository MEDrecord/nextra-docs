# Welcome to HealthTalk Documentation

HealthTalk is MEDrecord's secure healthcare communication platform designed for patient engagement and clinical workflows.

## What is HealthTalk?

HealthTalk enables healthcare providers to communicate securely with patients through multiple channels while maintaining full compliance with healthcare regulations including ISO 13485, ISO 62304, NEN 7510, and GDPR.

## Key Features

- **Secure Messaging** - End-to-end encrypted communication between patients and healthcare providers
- **Multi-Channel Support** - SMS, email, in-app notifications, and patient portal integration
- **Appointment Management** - Automated reminders, scheduling, and follow-ups
- **Template Library** - Pre-approved message templates for common clinical scenarios
- **Compliance Built-in** - Full audit trail and regulatory compliance documentation
- **API Integration** - Connect with your existing EHR, scheduling, and practice management systems
